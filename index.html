Welcome to Melbourne Eddie

Melbourne Eddie is a veterinary eye specialist centre at 9-11 Miles Street, Mulgrave. We have a purpose built, state of the art facility for treating patients with eye disease. Our philosophy is to provide a dignified, high quality service to referring veterinarians and their patients throughout Victoria. We specialist in treating eye conditions of all animals including dogs, cats, horses, rabbits, exotics, birds, wildlife and zoo animals. ​ We also visit Geelong, Essendon, Mill Park, Peninsula, Traralgon, Bendigo, Ballarat, Wodonga and Darwin. All bookings call on 03 9545 0103. Our hours are Monday–Friday 8 am–5:30 pm, Tuesday 8 am – 7 pm at Mulgrave.
